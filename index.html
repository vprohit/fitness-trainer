ABOUT ME


I'm a Fitness trainer from past 5 years and experience in Bodybuilding. I'm very passionate about helping others find happiness in life and feel good about themselves.

I believe that when you FEEL GOOD, you excel in life. For me, a healthy lifestyle is much more than just lifting weights.

I will instruct for individuals in exercise activities, demonstrate technique and form, observe participants, and explain to them corrective measures to improve their skills.
